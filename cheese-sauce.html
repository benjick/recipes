cheese sauce

  • 1.5 dl sunflower seeds
  • 1.5 dl water
  • 2 tablespoons nooch
  • 2 tablespoons citrus juice
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Pasta
  • Black papper
  • Salt to taste

Takes 1 hour, serves four people.

  1. Put the sunflower seeds in hot water for 30 minutes
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Add everything else in a blender
  4. Add some pasta water to the blender until you find a good thickness
