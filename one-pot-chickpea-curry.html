benjick's one pot chickpea curry

  • 2 boxes of chickpeas (460 grams drained and rinsed)
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes (~400 gram)
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 dl of brown rice
  • 3 small or 2 big onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of curry paste
  • 3 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • Salt to taste

Takes 1 hour, serves four people.

  1. Chop and saute the onions and the garlic
  2. Add the curry paste and let it blend with the onions
  3. Add everything else except the rice, bring to a boil and make sure the peanut butter is stirred well
  4. Add in the rice and let simmer for 45 minutes
  5. Stir once in a while
